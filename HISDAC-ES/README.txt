This folder contains the original information from HISDAC-ES (https://doi.org/10.5194/essd-15-4713-2023) which is the basis of the population disaggregation process.

The revised version dated 08-2023 is used, which is the one finally published.

The data are extracted from HISDAC-ES_All_LAEA_202308.zip, available at https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.22009643.

We use the layers for all of Spain in LAEA projection, Lambert Azimuthal Equal Area (EPSG:3035), on the themes of evolution for the years 1900, 1910, 1920, 1930, 1940, 1950, 1960, 1970, 1980, 1990, 2000, 2010 and 2020.

Variables: Evolution:
   resbia:  building indoor area of ​​residential buildings only
   resbufa: building footprint area of ​​residential buildings only
   bufa:    building footprint area
